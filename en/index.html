The signature feature

Wind and rain move across the map while the panel stays out of the way.

Animated arrows show wind direction and its effect on the rider, while the blue rain cell reveals the reach of precipitation. The panel folds away as you explore the map.

  • Colour-coded arrows show tailwind, crosswind, and headwind.
  • The blue rain cell shows precipitation reach and probability.
  • The compact panel keeps temperature, wind, rain, and distance visible.
  • 2D/3D views and replay move through the forecast point by point.
