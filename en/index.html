Risk map

Critical segments are drawn directly on the route

The strongest headwind does not disappear into a table. The map paints the problem segment red, labels the alert, and keeps a legend for cloud, rain, and headwind layers.

  • Cyan for the base route.
  • Red for headwind segments.
  • Orange for actions and replay.
  • Green for readable ride summaries.
