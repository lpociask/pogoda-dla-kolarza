The signature feature

Risk appears exactly where it happens on the route.

The map shows where rain begins and paints the sections exposed to a headwind. One glance tells you where the harder part of the day starts.

  • The blue route shows the complete ride.
  • A red segment marks the strongest headwind.
  • Weather markers highlight changing conditions.
  • Replay moves through the forecast point by point.
