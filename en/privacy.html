App Store privacy

Privacy Policy

Spin or Stay helps cyclists plan a ride with weather forecast points along a route. The app has no ads, does not use trackers, and does not sell user data.

Data used by the app

  • GPX/FIT files are read on-device to build the route, distance, and forecast points.
  • Route point coordinates may be sent to the weather providers selected in the app to fetch forecasts for the planned ride time.
  • PDF reports are generated locally on the device.
  • The current App Store version does not require an account.

Weather sources

The app can use Apple Weather through WeatherKit, Open-Meteo, and MET Norway. Data sent to these services is limited to coordinates and forecast times needed for app functionality.

Storage and sharing

  • The app developer does not operate a server that stores user route data.
  • Routes imported by the user remain local unless the user shares them outside the app.
  • Weather data is not used to track users and is not linked to an advertising identifier.

User control

Local app data can be removed by deleting the app from the device. Users can choose a weather source in the app and refresh the forecast only when needed.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact lpociask@me.com.
