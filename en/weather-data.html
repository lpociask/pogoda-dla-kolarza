Weather Data

Spin or Stay shows weather in the context of a ride. Forecasts are fetched for route points and the time when the cyclist is expected to reach them.

Apple Weather Apple Weather data is available through WeatherKit. When the app displays Apple Weather data, it shows Weather attribution and links to legal attribution.
Open-Meteo Open-Meteo provides forecasts through an HTTPS API. Its data requires attribution and is subject to Open-Meteo terms, including limits and commercial-use rules.
MET Norway MET Norway Locationforecast provides forecasts for coordinates. The app identifies itself with a User-Agent that includes the app name and technical contact.
Demo Demo mode generates sample weather locally so the screens can be tested without fetching a network forecast.

What is sent

Weather providers receive route point coordinates and forecast times. The app does not send the full GPX/FIT file to the app developer.
